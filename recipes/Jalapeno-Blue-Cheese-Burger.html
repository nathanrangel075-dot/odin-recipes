Jalapeno Blue Cheese Burger

This is a recipe for making a Jalapeno Blue Cheese burger. This recipe provides an easy step-by-step to make and eat and a very hot and delicious burger. Jalapeno burgers are the best way to enjoy the tastiness of a burger. You wouldn't even have to go to a restaurant after making this. Enjoy this meal but don't too fast or the spicyness will get to you!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 6 jalapeno peppers, seeded, and chopped
  • 8 ounces crumbled blue cheese
  • 2 tablespoons onion powder
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons tamari or soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
  • 4 slices Swiss cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, split

Steps to make a Jalapeno-Blue-Cheese-Burger

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat a grill for high heat

  2. Step 2

    3. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, jalapeno peppers, blue cheese, onion powder, garlic powder, tamari and salt. Mix well using your hands. Pat lightly into 4 large fat patties.

  3. Step 3

    4. Place patties on the grill, and cook for about 8 minutes per side, or until well done. Place a slice of cheese on top of each patty, and cover the grill until the cheese melts. Serve on buns with your favorite toppings.
