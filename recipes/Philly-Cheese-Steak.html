Philly Cheese Steak Sandwich

One of the best sandwiches you can ever eat and with this recipe, you can make it at home! This sandwich will blow your mind and make you want to make more of it to the point of no return. This recipe provides the necessary ingredients and easy steps in making a Philly cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh steak (rib-eye, round, or sirloin)
  • 1 large yellow onion, sliced thin
  • 3 tablespoons kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade and Sauce
  • 4 soft sandwich rolls
  • 1 (8 fl oz) jar cheese spread

Steps to make a Philly Cheese Steak

  1. Step 1

    2. Add oil to a large non-stick skillet. Saute onions and 2 tablespoons of teriyaki sauce until the onions are soft.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add the sliced meat and cook until meat browns slightly, add remaining 1 tablespoon of teriyaki sauce and stir to combine

  3. Step 3

    4. Place cheese on the meat and stir until melted, mixing meat, onions and cheese together. Scoop meat mixture onto sandwich rolls.
